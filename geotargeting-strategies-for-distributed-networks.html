Geotargeting Strategies for Distributed Networks

Publikoval Miroslav Procházka | 17. 07. 2026 | ⏰ 1 min čtení

Regional relevance is essential. When deploying a network of resources, attaching precise geographic tags (such as ICBM coordinates and Dublin Core schemas) helps crawlers understand the resource.

By switching local business schemas across deployments, organizations can mimic organic geographic distribution without maintaining physical servers in those areas.
